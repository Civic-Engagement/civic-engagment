Step 2: Your Rights as a Contract Member

Your Democratic Powers:

  • Vote in Elections: Choose representatives at all levels

  • Run for Office: Start at city level, advance through experience
  • Appeal Decisions: Challenge unfair treatment through due process
  • Propose Changes: Submit ideas for platform improvements

Step 3: How Elections Work

City Elections - Your Starting Point

Who Can Run: Any registered Contract Member
Who Votes: All members in your city
When: When 1% of city population joins (first) or 50% participate (ongoing)
Term: 1 year, maximum 4 terms total (cannot be consecutive, 1-year break required)

Higher Level Elections

State Level: Must have city experience, cities vote via electoral college
Country Level: Must have state experience, states vote via electoral college
World Level: Must have country experience, countries vote via electoral college

Step 4: Understanding Representation

Population-Based Scaling:

  • City: 2 Senators + 2+ Representatives (varies by population)
  • State: 2 Senators + Representatives (1 per 500K people, min 2)
  • Country: 2 Senators + Representatives (1 per 1M people, min 2)
  • World: 2 Senators + Representatives (1 per 4M people, min 2)

Step 7: Understanding Safeguards

Your Protections:

  • Term Limits: No one can accumulate too much power
  • Electoral College: Small communities get fair representation
  • Blockchain Transparency: All votes and decisions are public
  • Appeal Process: Challenge unfair treatment
  • Constitutional Review: Elder oversight prevents abuse
