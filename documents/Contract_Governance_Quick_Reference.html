Common Questions

Q: Is this a replacement for my government?
A: No! This is separate platform governance. You keep all regular rights.

Q: Can anyone really run for office?
A: Yes at city level! Higher levels require progressive experience.

Q: How secure is my vote?
A: Bank-level cryptographic security with blockchain verification.

Q: How much time does it take?
A: Voting takes minutes. Running for office requires more engagement.

Q: What prevents corruption?
A: Term limits, transparency, appeals, and constitutional oversight.
